Family Oriented Music Organization

Family Oriented Music Organization has been in business since 1965. Our core values include providing families in the community with access to quality instruments and musical instruction.

We offer the best deal in Band and Orchestra Rent-To-Own Programs in the state. We carry a fantastic selection of guitars, both acoustic and electric with over 200 different models. We carry an extensive sheet music library with both lesson books and popular titles.

We will do our very best to keep our family values and offer premium service and products.

We hope that you have an enjoyable experience on our site and in our stores. We strive to help all families have access to the amazing power of music in their homes.
